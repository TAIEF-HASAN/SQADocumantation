<h3>Types Of Software Testing</h3>

Here are the software Testing Type:

Typically Testing is clasified into three categories.

- **Functional Testing**
- **Non-Functional Testing or Performance Testing**
- **Maintenance (Regration and Maintence)**

![alt text](image.png)

Sub Type of <strong>Functional</strong> testing:

- **Unit Testing**
- **Integration Testing**
- **Smoke**
- **UAT (Acceptance Testing)**
- **Localization**
- **Globalization**
- **Interoperability**
- **So on**

Sub Type of <strong>Non-Functional</strong> testing:

- **Performance**
- **Enduance**
- **Load**
- **Volume**
- **Scalability**
- **Usability**
- **So on**

Sub Type of <strong>Maintenance</strong> testing:

- **Regression**
- **Maintenance**

This is not the complete list as there are more than 150 types of testing types and still adding. Also, note that not all testing types are applicable to all projects but depend on the nature & scope of the project.
